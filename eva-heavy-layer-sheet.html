EVA Heavy Layer Sheet — mass that forms to the car.

A thermoformable, high-density EVA barrier built for automotive noise insulation. It adds limp mass where road, engine and exhaust noise enters — and heat-forms to floors, firewalls and arches that flat barriers can't follow.

Request a quote → Jump to specs
How it works

Barrier mass, with the ability to be moulded.

Like all heavy-layer barriers, the EVA sheet works by mass law — a dense, limp layer that reflects and absorbs airborne sound. What sets it apart is the EVA carrier: heat it and it becomes pliable, so it can be vacuum- or press-formed to the exact shape of a floor pan, transmission tunnel or wheel arch and then holds that shape as it cools.

That makes it a natural fit for OEM and aftermarket automotive parts — dash insulators, floor insulators, parcel trays and arch liners — where a flat sheet would gap and leak noise.

Where it works

Built for the cabin.

The EVA Heavy Layer Sheet is mainly specified for automotive and vehicle noise control, on its own or laminated to foam and felt.
