The material

A heavy, flexible barrier that turns sound into nothing.

Mass loaded vinyl works on a simple principle of physics — mass blocks sound. AcoustiLayer® is a high-density vinyl sheet at ~2000 kg/m³ that adds limp mass exactly where you need it, without the bulk of traditional barriers.

01

Dense, limp-mass core

Mineral-loaded vinyl stays flexible at any thickness, conforming to panels, studs, vehicle floors and ductwork without cracking.

02

Thin where space is tight

At 2–5 mm, AcoustiLayer® adds barrier-grade mass in the thickness of a few coins — built for wall cavities, ceilings, cabins and ductwork.

03

Water-resistant & durable

A black, water-resistant vinyl that pairs with mineral wool or acoustic foam for higher-performing assemblies.

FAQ

Mass loaded vinyl, answered.

Common questions about AcoustiLayer® MLV — what it is, how it performs and how to install it.

What is mass loaded vinyl (MLV)?

Mass loaded vinyl (MLV) is a dense, flexible limp-mass barrier that blocks airborne noise. Super Autotech's AcoustiLayer® MLV is a high-density vinyl sheet at 2000 kg/m³ that adds sound-blocking mass to walls, ceilings, vehicles, ducts and machinery — without the bulk of traditional barriers.

What thicknesses and sizes does AcoustiLayer® MLV come in?

AcoustiLayer® mass loaded vinyl is available in 2 mm, 3 mm, 4 mm and 5 mm thicknesses. It ships in black, in standard 5 m × 1 m rolls, with custom cut sizes and widths available to order.

Where is mass loaded vinyl used?

AcoustiLayer® MLV is used for wall and partition soundproofing, ceiling systems, automotive and vehicle cabins, acoustic fencing, HVAC duct lining, equipment noise control, and machinery and generator enclosures.

How is mass loaded vinyl installed?

AcoustiLayer® MLV is installed behind gypsum board or panels as a concealed barrier, fixed in place using construction adhesive or mechanical fasteners. It can be combined with mineral wool or acoustic foam to improve assembly performance.

Is AcoustiLayer® mass loaded vinyl water resistant?

Yes. AcoustiLayer® MLV is a water-resistant, durable black vinyl barrier suitable for construction, automotive, HVAC and industrial environments.
