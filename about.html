About us

Twenty years making the barrier, not reselling it.

Super Autotech is an Indian manufacturer of mass loaded vinyl. Since 2006 we've controlled the material, the density and the lead time of AcoustiLayer® - and that control is why customers across construction, automotive and industry come back.

From a single product, done properly

We focus on one thing: dense, consistent mass loaded vinyl. Two decades of making the same product means a formulation and a process we trust - and that you can build on, lot after lot.

Because we manufacture in-house, we can offer stock grades that ship fast, custom sizes and slit widths, and private-label rolls for OEM and distributor partners. When you have a question about density, assembly or lead time, you're talking to the people who actually make the material.
